# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Pharma/biotech partners, investors, and clinical investigators evaluating CinPressa Pharma. They arrive from CinRx's site, a pitch deck, or a search after a press release. They are scientifically literate, time-poor, and judging credibility: is this program real, well-run, and worth a conversation?

## Product Purpose

A single-page credibility splash for CinPressa Pharma, an asset-focused CinCo advancing a cardiometabolic therapeutic toward IND within the CinRx Pharma portfolio. Success = a visitor understands the asset, the stage, and the operating model in under a minute, then clicks through to CinRx partnering.

## Brand Personality

Precise, calm, confident. Clinical-grade restraint with one moment of warmth (the orange punctuation). Reads like a well-run program, not a startup pitch.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing patterns: hero-metric grids, identical icon cards, gradient text.
- Biotech stock clichés: gloved hands, pipettes, DNA helix renders.
- Dark "tech startup" aesthetics; this brand lives in daylight blues on white.
- Anything loud. Urgency, countdowns, exclamation marks.

## Design Principles

1. **The hairline is the brand.** 1px lines, dashed orbits, and rules carry the visual system; never thick decorative borders.
2. **Palette is locked.** Colors come from the official CINPRESSA Logo Spec Sheet (core blue #2261AD, sky #3AAED8, orange #F9A81A). Never reinterpret; grays are derived support only.
3. **Orange is punctuation only.** One accent moment per view, never a surface.
4. **Motion is orbital and unhurried.** Slow rotations, soft rises, exponential ease-outs. Nothing bounces; nothing rushes the reader.
5. **One asset, one page, one message.** Every element earns its place or gets cut.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA contrast for all text. `prefers-reduced-motion` fully honored (animations neutralized, content always visible without JS). Keyboard focus visible on all interactive elements.
